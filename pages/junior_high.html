Junior High School

Overview

Develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and values while preparing students for senior high tracks. Grades 7-10 focus on advanced subjects, leadership, and personal growth.

Curriculum Highlights

• Commits to quality Catholic education with spirituality, excellence, and service as core themes.

• Integrates "Ways of Life" and 21st-century skills framework.

• Strengthens faith life and spirituality among students and faculty.

• Aims for well-rounded development addressing faith, academics, and social skills.
