Senior High School

Overview

Choose specialized tracks such as Academic, Technical-Vocational, and Arts & Design for career readiness. Grades 11-12 prepare students for higher education or workforce entry.

Curriculum Highlights

• Embodies the theme "Parish and School: Journeying in Joyful Mission."

• Focuses on Catholic education through enriched faith and values integration.

• Offers curriculum maps focused on moral and civic education and relevant college preparation.

• Promotes a joyful mission of Catholic service and community.

Academic Track 1

ABM - For students interested in business, management, accountancy, and entrepreneurship.

STEM - For those who want to pursue studies and careers in science, engineering, medical and technological fields.

HUMSS - Ideal for students inclined towards social sciences, education, liberal arts, and related fields.

Academic Track 2

Arts & Design Track - Focused on visual arts, design, culture, and creativity for students aspiring for careers in creative industries.

TechVoc - Home Economics - For careers in hospitality, tourism, culinary arts, bread and pastry, and related practical skills.

TechVoc - ICT - For students interested in computer systems, programming, animation, and digital technologies.
