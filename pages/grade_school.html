Grade School

Overview

Comprehensive curriculum emphasizing literacy, numeracy, arts, and values formation for young learners. Our program builds strong academic foundations and character development from Grades 1 to 6.

Curriculum Highlights

• Focuses on holistic and integrated development of the person.

• Aims to produce Christ-like graduates equipped with knowledge, skills, and values to meet contemporary challenges.

• Supports the vision, mission, and educational objectives of the school.

• Emphasizes the formation of mind, body, and spirit.

• Offers programs and activities to strengthen Catholic values and character.
